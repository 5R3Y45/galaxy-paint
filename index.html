6 Steps to Achieve the Best Results in Wall Painting

Follow these essential steps to ensure a flawless painting experience and long-lasting, beautiful walls.

Preparation
Set up a "crime scene" to protect furniture and surfaces, saving cleaning time later. Cover furniture and tape off edges.
Inspect & Prepare Substrate
Ensure walls are clean, dry, and stable. Scrape off old, loose paint and let new plaster cure for at least 4 weeks.
Address Cracks & Imperfections
Repair cracks or bulges with stucco or sealant. Remove stains, grease, and dirt using soapy water as needed.
Apply Penetrating Primer
Strengthen walls with primer to improve paint adhesion, even out absorption, and reduce paint use.
Painting Technique
Start with edges using a brush, then roll paint in overlapping stripes from windows. Apply two coats for a flawless finish.
Final Touches
Ensure smooth finish by applying a second coat, and touch up any imperfections.
